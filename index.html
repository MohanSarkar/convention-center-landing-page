Frequently Asked Questions

Event design and event planning both goes hand in hand for a successful event. Whether it is a book fair or a wedding program the event is planned according to the event design on the chosen theme.

The event planning should start as soon as the date of the event has been fixed.

We refund the total advanced amount if we are notified of the program cancellation at least 1 month before the scheduled event. Less then 1 month includes a 10% deduction of the advanced amount.

Our expert team always keep tabs on all expenses and provides detailed info on the expenses and plans according to client's budget.
